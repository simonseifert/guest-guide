For hot water, the **blue light** on the switch near the downstairs bathroom **must always be on**.

## 🌡️ Temperature

On the boiler itself, on the **blue LED display**, you can raise or lower the temperature with the **+ / −** buttons.

**Maximum temperature: 65 °C.** Recommended: **~60 °C** — hot enough for a shower, efficient, and safe from scalding.

⚠️ Always test the water before use, especially when bathing children.

## 🌿 ECO EVO mode

The boiler has an **ECO EVO function** (button on the panel) — it learns your hot-water habits and only heats when needed. Saves electricity and reduces standby losses.

- ECO learns over **about 4 weeks** — temperature can fluctuate at first.
- It's **normal for temperature to drop** between uses (e.g. from 60 °C to 40 °C) — ECO is waiting for the next predicted heating moment.
- **Please leave ECO on.** If you switch it off, the water heats more often and consumption goes up.

## ⏳ If the water runs cold

The boiler holds **100 L** and the heating element is **1500 W**.

- For **partial recovery** (one more short shower): **30-60 min**.
- For a **full reheat from cold** (to ~60 °C): **2-3 hours**.

After two long showers in a row, give it some time before the next one.

## ⚠️ If the whole display is flashing

This means the boiler has hit a fault.

1. **Switch the boiler off at the mains** for 1 minute.
2. Switch it back on.
3. If it's still flashing, **contact us** — don't try to open the unit yourself.
