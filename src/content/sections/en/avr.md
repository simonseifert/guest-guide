The music system is a stereo receiver / amplifier wired to the speakers in the room. Most modern receivers work the same way — these instructions are deliberately generic. _Replace with your model's specifics._

## ▶️ Power on/off

- Press the **Power** button on the front panel or the remote.
- An indicator light shows when the unit is on.

## 🎚️ Volume

- Use the **large volume dial** on the front (or the remote).
- Raise it slowly — most receivers have plenty of power and can get loud quickly.

## 🎛️ Selecting an input

Use the **Input Selector** to choose:

- **Bluetooth** — stream from your phone (see below)
- **Tuner** — FM/AM radio
- **CD / Aux** — analog inputs

The current input is shown on the front display.

## 🔵 Bluetooth — pairing your phone

1. Turn the **Input Selector** to **Bluetooth** (or press **BT** on the remote).
2. The display will show **"Pairing"**.
3. On your phone, open **Bluetooth settings** and choose **\[your receiver's name\]**.
4. Wait a few seconds — when the display shows your phone's name, you're connected.
5. Play music as usual — it'll come through the speakers.

Next time, if the receiver is on and Bluetooth selected, your phone reconnects automatically.

## 🔇 If there's no sound

- Check the unit isn't **muted** (Mute button on the remote).
- Check the **right input is selected**.
- Check the **speakers are switched on** at the receiver.
