The music system is an **Onkyo TX-8220** — a stereo receiver (amplifier) with Bluetooth and an FM/AM radio tuner. It's wired to the speakers in the room.

## ▶️ Power on/off

- Press the **🔘 Power button** on the front panel or the remote.
- The indicator light around the button turns on when the unit is on.

## 🎚️ Volume

- The **large round knob on the right** (Volume) — turn to change volume.
- Raise it slowly — the unit is powerful (100 W per channel) and can get loud before you expect it.

## 🎛️ Selecting an input

Left of the volume knob is a **small Input Selector dial** — turn it to choose:

- **NET / BT** — Bluetooth (see below)
- **TUNER** — FM/AM radio
- **CD / GAME / AUX / PHONO** — analog inputs (CD player, turntable, etc.)

The current input is shown on the **front display**.

## 🔵 Bluetooth — pairing your phone

1. Turn the **Input Selector** to **NET/BT** (or press **BT** on the remote).
2. The display will show **"Pairing"**.
3. On your phone, open **Bluetooth settings** and choose **"Onkyo TX-8220"**.
4. Wait a few seconds — when the display shows your phone's name, you're connected.
5. Play music as usual — it'll come through the speakers.

Next time, if the receiver is on and BT selected, your phone reconnects automatically if it's in range.

## 🔇 If there's no sound

- Check the unit isn't **muted** (Mute button on the remote — the display shows *"Muting"*).
- Check the **right input is selected** (Input Selector).
- Check the **speakers are on** — the **Speaker A** button must be pressed in (light on).
- If you're using the TV via the AUX cable, the TV audio won't come through the receiver unless you select AUX.
