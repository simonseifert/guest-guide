## 🤫 Peace and quiet

Please respect quiet hours **from 22:00 to 08:00**.

Parties and larger gatherings are not allowed without prior approval from the host.

## 🚭 Smoking

Smoking is allowed **only outdoors**.

## 🛏️ Additional guests

Daytime visits, extended stays, or overnight visits from **additional guests** (friends, family…) are **NOT ALLOWED** and will be charged separately, depending on whether it's a day or night stay.

In the event of an inspection, the registered guest is responsible for any fines incurred for unregistered persons.

## 💦 Pool

- Use the pool **at your own risk**.
- Children must be **under constant adult supervision**.
- No **jumping or running** around the pool.
- **No glass items** in the pool area.

Full details in the [Hot tub](/en/jacuzzi) section.

## 🕯️ Safety

- Don't light candles inside the cabin — only outdoors, in the candle holders provided.
- Keep the front door and terrace door closed (or pull the screen) to keep out flying and other animals. 🦟🐝🐭
- When leaving the cabin, **turn off the lights** and air-conditioning.
- Keep the keys in a safe place.
- Report any damage or fault **to the host immediately**.

## 🐾 Outdoors

Don't leave shoes, clothing, or food outside overnight — they may not be there in the morning. 🦊🐾

## 💚 Furniture and equipment

Please look after the furniture and equipment as if it were your own.
