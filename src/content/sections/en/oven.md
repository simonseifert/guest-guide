## 🕐 Clock flashing?

Press the **clock button briefly 4 times** until the icon flashes, then **turn the knob** to set the time. It locks in by itself after 10 s.

## 🍳 Cooking

Left dial = **function**, right dial = **temperature**.

- **Conventional** (top + bottom heater) — bread, cakes, pizza
- **Fan (convection)** — set **20-30 °C lower** than conventional
- **Grill** — browning (max 250 °C)
- **ECO** — energy-saving; **don't preheat, don't open the door** during cooking
- **Defrost** — fan only

**R light** = oven is on. **L light** = heater is drawing power; goes out when the set temp is reached. *Cycling on/off is normal.*

## 🔥 First use

Wash the trays and racks, put them back in, then run **conventional at 250 °C / 30 min** empty with a window open. Light smoke or smell is normal — protective coatings burning off.

## 🛡️ Safety

- **The door and glass get very hot** — keep children back.
- When opening, **hot steam escapes** — pull slowly, face back.
- Don't lean on the open door; don't place heavy items on it (>15 kg).
- The **cooling fan** may run for a few minutes after baking — that's normal.
- Door glass: damp cloth + mild detergent. **No abrasives or metal scrapers** — the glass can crack.
