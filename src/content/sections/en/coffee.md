## ▶️ Starting up

1. Make sure the machine is plugged in.
2. Fill the **water tank** if it's low.
3. Insert a capsule into the holder.
4. Press the **main power button** and wait for the ready light.
5. Place a cup under the spout and press the **brew button**.

## 🔧 If no coffee comes out

After long periods of non-use, the capsule slot can clog.

- Remove the capsule.
- Run **a few cups of plain hot water** through to flush.
- ⚠️ Careful — the water is hot.

Then re-insert a capsule and try again. If no water comes out at all, check that the **water tank is properly seated** (it should click into place).

## 🚨 Warning lights

If you see a red or flashing warning light, **don't keep pressing buttons** — message us. Most are easy to fix once we know what's happening.

## ⚠️ Steam wand

If your machine has a steam / milk wand, don't touch it during use — it gets very hot.
