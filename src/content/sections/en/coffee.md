The machine is an **Illy X7.1 Iperespresso** — it uses Illy Iperespresso capsules. The manufacturer's full guide is in the **grey envelope on the bar**.

## ▶️ Starting up

1. Plug the machine into the mains.
2. Fill the **blue water tank**.
3. Insert a capsule into the holder.
4. Press the **main switch on the left** until the yellow 💛 light comes on.
5. Wait a few minutes for the machine to reach operating temperature — the blue 💙 cup-icon light will come on.
6. Place a cup under the spout and press the **blue cup icon** to brew.

## 🔧 If no coffee comes out

After long periods of standing or non-use, the capsule can clog.

- Remove the handle with the capsule (or just the capsule).
- Run **a few cups of water** through to flush.
- ⚠️ Careful — the water is hot, watch out for burns.

Then re-insert a capsule and try again.

If no water comes out at all, check that the **blue water tank is properly seated** (it should "click" into place).

## 🚨 Red light

If a **red light** appears on the machine, **don't keep pressing buttons** — message Selma or Marija.

## ⚠️ Watch out for the steam wand (Pannarello)

The machine has a **steam / milk wand** on the side. If you're not using it, don't touch it — it gets very hot (around 120 °C) and can cause burns.
