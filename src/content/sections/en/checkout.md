## ⏰ Check-out time

**Check-out:** by **12:00**.

## 💶 Late check-out

If you'd like to extend your stay, let us know in advance — we can usually arrange it for an additional fee:

- **Until 16:00** — fee **60 EUR**
- **Until 19:00** — fee **120 EUR**

## 🔐 Security deposit

The **200 EUR security deposit** (collected at check-in) is **refunded in full** if no damage is found on the day of departure.

If damage is found, the deposit may be partially withheld (up to the value of the damage assessed) or in full.

## ✅ Before you leave

- Turn off lights, air-conditioning, and all appliances.
- Replace the cover on the hot tub.
- Leave the keys in the agreed place.
- Double-check you haven't left any personal items behind. 🎒
