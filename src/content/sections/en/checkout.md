## ⏰ Check-out time

**Check-out:** by **11:00**.

## 💶 Late check-out

If you'd like to extend your stay, let us know in advance — we can usually arrange it for an additional fee. _Set your own rates here._

## 🔐 Security deposit

The security deposit collected at check-in is **refunded in full** if no damage is found on the day of departure. If damage is found, the deposit may be partially or fully withheld.

## ✅ Before you leave

- Turn off lights, air-conditioning, and all appliances.
- Replace the cover on the hot tub (if your property has one).
- Leave the keys in the agreed place.
- Double-check you haven't left any personal items behind. 🎒
