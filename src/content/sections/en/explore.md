Experience Gorski kotar in a different way. Here's our list — from places at the cabin's doorstep to full-day trips.

> ⚠️ **Mobile signal** can be patchy in the forest. Download the map for offline use in Google Maps before you go — Settings → Offline Maps.

## 🥾 Walking distance / under 15 min drive

- **Zeleni vir & Vražji prolaz** (Skrad) — a canyon with wooden boardwalks, a waterfall, and a cave. The most beautiful walk in the area, and literally next door. *[More info](https://zelenivir.com.hr/en/)*
- **Gates of Gorski kotar** — viewpoint overlooking forest and gorges, perfect for photos.

## 🚗 Up to 30 min drive

- **Forest Park Golubinjak** (Lokve) — trail through ancient forest, "Queen of the Forest" — a 500-year-old fir tree. Easy, great for kids.
- **Lake Lokve** (Lokve) — swimming in summer, boat rides, walking trails around the lake.
- **Kamačnik canyon** (Vrbovsko) — wooden boardwalks along a stream, ending at an emerald spring.
- **Petehovac** (Delnice) — viewpoint, sledging in winter.

## 🏞️ Up to 1h drive

- **Risnjak National Park** — untouched wilderness, hiking trails to the summit (1528 m).
- **Bijele and Samarske Stijene** — strict nature reserve, dramatic karst formations.
- **Snježnik** — alternative to Risnjak, less busy.
- **Source of the Kupa river** — emerald spring, source of one of Croatia's most beautiful rivers.
- **Čelimbaša ski resort** (Mrkopalj) — winter skiing + sledging at "Gorski sjaj".

## 🍽️ Where to eat

- **Volta** (Fužine) — refined local cooking, seasonal menu.
- **Bitoraj** (Fužine) — institution for game and traditional dishes. *If you only try one Gorski kotar restaurant, make it this one.*
- **Mali raj** (Skrad) — home-style cooking, near the cabin.

**Local specialties:** frog legs 🐸, dormouse stew (puh) 🐭, game (venison, wild boar), wild mushrooms, forest berries, local cheeses.

## ☔ If it rains

- **Volta** or **Bitoraj** — long dinner with wine 🍷
- **Crna jezera in Lokve** — cave (seasonal)
- **Risnjak visitor centre** (Crni Lug) — interactive exhibit on the forest and bears
- At home: 🛀 hot tub + 🧖 sauna + something on the **Onkyo music system** 🎵

## 🍂 By season

**Spring (March–May)** — hiking through the awakening forest, kayaking on the Kupa river (Brod na Kupi), first porcini 🍄.

**Summer (June–September)** — swimming at **Lake Lokve** and **Bajer** in Fužine; the **Adriatic** at **Rijeka** and **Opatija** (~1h drive); biking the Gorski kotar bike network; **adventure park Vrata** (Fužine) with zipline; **Frog Legs Festival** (Fužine, July).

**Autumn (September–November)** — peak mushroom season 🍄 (porcini, chanterelles); **Days of Wild Game** in Skrad; forest colours; **Risnjak Trail** race.

**Winter (December–March)**

- **Skiing at Platak** (~1h drive) — the nearest proper ski resort, with a view of the sea
- **Čelimbaša ski resort** (Mrkopalj) — less busy, good for beginners
- **Sledging** at Petehovac and "Gorski sjaj" (Mrkopalj)
- **Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing** — Petehovac, Begovo Razdolje
- **Advent** in **Delnice** and **Fužine** — Christmas markets, mulled wine, live nativity scenes
- **Ice rink** in Delnice (in season)
